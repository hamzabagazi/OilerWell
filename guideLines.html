Guidelines

* As a reminder, to be eligible for the $25/month Health Server Discount, participants need to meet the following:

  • All: One Adult Tetanus with pertussis (every 10 years)
  • All: Non-Smoking
  • Men Age 50 and Over: Screening colonoscopy (we can help make appointments) Annual physical exam including prostate health discussion (as directed by your physician)
  • Women Age 50 and Over: Screening colonoscopy (we can help make appointments)
  • Women Age 40 and Over: Annual mammogram
  • Women Age 21 and Over: Annual exam with screening PAP (as directed by your physician)

*Make note of your dates. You will need to provide them the day of your screening.
